Train with what works

My purpose is to get you fit as hell, not to push some workout ideology on you. I don't train meatheads. I don't do Jane Fonda bullshit either. Instead I evolve my modern fitness systems based on empirical data and kinesiological research.

They combine classic strength and conditioning (barbells, dumbbells, exercise bike) with functional work (kettlebells, resistance bands, slide boards).

Want to lose weight? Want to pick up some muscle? Want to look better in the mirror? It all goes into the formula. Your program will be comprehensive health, fitness, and strength you can use. And it's the best way to reach your goals.
