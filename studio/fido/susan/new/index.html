Welcome

Everyone we've ever met who has a dog wants them to be better trained. Well-behaved dogs are safer, more confident, happier, and easier for anyone to be around.

But until now, our only options were years behind the products we use in the rest of our lives. That makes it tough for most of us to stick it out long enough to see the results we want.

So we thought, what if we tried designing a dog training system from scratch, that's as simple and fun for people like us as it is useful and productive for our dogs?

This is a prototype lesson from an app we might call Fido. It's designed around habit: a few, short, fun bursts of training on most days. It's based on research from canine behaviorists and human behavioral designers.

We're going to experiment using Fido for a few days. This prototype requires as little as 7 minutes, twice a day, for 3 days. Can you make that time for your dog?
