Training Sessions

  • My human is a stubborn one

    With food in hand and some patience, we’ll start teaching impulse control.

  • A twist: they’re on the floor

    Showing progress, we move the food from in hand to the floor and press on.

  • Is he, is he paying attention?

    Now we move ourselves farther away from the stash and test his control.

  • I can almost touch them!

    Move the stash closer to his paws to forge his resolve before the big test.

  • Premack Principle

  • Final Boss: It’s…on my paw

    Timer running, we place treats on his paw for this final showdown.
