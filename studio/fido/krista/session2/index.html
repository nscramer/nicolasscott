Let’s get started

Fistful of deliciousness

Grab a handful of food. Hold it tightly in your fist so he can smell it but can’t get it.

Start easy

Begin the session with food in hand again. Start Axle out winning to build confidence.

Take it low

Now put the food onto the floor. Cover it with your hand. Let him investigate.

No need to rush

If he’s struggling, go back to the hand for a few repetitions. Then once he’s back on his game, try again.

Reward restraint

Just like with the food in hand, reward his patience and restraint. He’s learning!
