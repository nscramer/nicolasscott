Impulse Control

Attack of the hungry puppy

Your dog will smell the food and start to go after your hand. Keep still and let him sniff, lick, and nudge. Don't yell, tell him no, or discourage him.

Patience = an opening

As soon as he pauses his attack, slowly open up your hand. When he rushes back towards your hand, close it again before he gets a taste. Rinse and repeat.

Bingo!

Your dog ain't dumb. He'll figure out that your hand stays open when he leans back. And once he does, bam! Reward him right away with the other hand. Even if it was just for an instant, give him some of that deliciousness. Om nom nom.
