Everyone we’ve ever met who has a dog wants them to be better trained.

Well-behaved dogs are safer, more confident, happier, and easier for anyone to be around.

But our only training options are years behind the products we use in the rest of our lives.

That makes it tough for most of us to stick it out long enough to see the results we want.

So we thought, what if we tried designing a dog training system from scratch?

What if it’s as simple and fun for people like us as it is useful and productive for our dogs?

This is a prototype lesson from an app we might build.

It’s all about habit—a couple short, fun bursts of training on most days.

We based it on research from canine behaviorists and human behavioral designers.

We’re going to experiment using it for a few days.

It’ll take as little as 5 minutes a day for 5 days.
